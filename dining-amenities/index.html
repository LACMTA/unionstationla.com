Dining & Amenities

Complimenting the renowned Traxx Restaurant, one of the city’s most unique formal culinary experiences, the station is in the midst of opening new on-the-go eateries encompassing tastes from around the globe. Also scheduled to launch is an extensive new retail experience in the Fall, featuring curated selections of artists and artisans from across the Southland.

Dodger’s Express

On game days, shuttles to Dodger Stadium pick up in front of the Fred Harvey Restaurant (on the west end of the station) beginning 1.5 hours before game time. The service continues for up to an hour following the game.

Hollywood Bowl Shuttle

Pick-up and drop off is located at the Amtrak Bus Plaza from June 18th to July 11th (Spot #5) After July 11th it will be located behind the Amtrak building in the shuttle loading and unloading area.

Bike Share

Metro and the City of Los Angeles are launching Metro’s bike sharing program on Thursday, July 7, 2016. Up to 1,000 bicycles will be available at up to 65 downtown L.A. locations including Union Station.

To use the bike share system, customers can purchase a monthly or annual flex pass at http://www.metro.net/bikeshare.
