Happenings

The station hosts a rotating collection of unique events, cutting edge performances, evocative music, unprecedented art exhibitions and thought-provoking cultural programing showcasing the diversity of entertainers, artists and innovators throughout Los Angeles.

Upcoming Events

Bike Night

An off-the-chain celebration of L.A. cyclists and friends filled with live music, dancing, food trucks, raffles, games and more. Pedal over or come by train or bus to this lively downtown party in the Station’s Historic Ticket Hall.

Friday, May 27, Throughout the Evening, Historic Ticket Hall

The Floor Improv Night

Metro Art hosts a one-of-a kind dance party featuring a diverse group of L.A. dancers and musicians who will fuse their unique styles at the Station’s Historic ticket Hall for a live improvised Jam session featuring swing, jazz, tap and tango.

Sunday, June 12, 12:00 p.m. – 4:00 p.m., Historic Ticket Hall

Charles Phoenix

Well-known pop culture humorist, historian and author in a live performance of the retro slide show “Downtown Los Angelesland,” accompanied by his signature comedic commentary at a Metro Art event that’s sure to inspire Angelenos to rediscover the heart and soul of the city.

Friday, June 17, 7:00 p.m. – 8:30 p.m, Historic Ticket Hall

Coming this Summer

Union Station 360

A spectacular immersive nighttime journey into the dream of Los Angeles, past, present and future via a symphony of dazzling multimedia imagery and hypnotic soundscapes.

Union Station/Live Nation Emerging Artists Series

Eight weekends of open air concerts featuring some of music’s newest stars under the stars in the Station’s famed South Patio gardens.

Cabinet of Curiosities

A stunning and imaginative exhibition of Los Angeles-based artists featuring a series of mysterious worlds that are viewed through peepholes.
